&7- &6&l/tpr&r&f, teleport randomly
&7- &6&l/back&r&f, teleport to your last location
&7- &6&l/afk&r&f, toggle AFK status
&7- &6&l/givepet&r&f, transfer a pet
&7- &6&l/nick&r&f, set nickname
&7- &6&l/realname&r&f, view a player's real username
&7- &6&l/pronouns&r&f, set your pronouns or view others

&7- &b&l/trustlist&r&f, view trusted players in your claim
&7- &b&l/listclaims&r&f, view all of your claims
&7- &b&l/extendclaim&r&f, extend your claim in the direction you're facing
&7- &b&l/abandonclaim&r&f, abandon the claim you're standing in
&7- &b&l/gpflags&r&f, modify flags in your claim
&7- &b&l/flaghelp&r&f, display flags available to all players
